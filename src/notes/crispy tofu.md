## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
- 2 tbsp cornstarch
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- Vegetable oil
- OPTIONAL: 2 tsp minced garlic

## Recipe

1. Cut tofu into 1 inch cubes.
	- They should all be the same size so they fry at the same rate
2. Sift flour, starch and baking powder into a large plate.
	- Add salt and pepper seasoning then
3. Use a paper towel *gently* to pat dry the tofu cubes
4. Put the tofu cubes into the mixture
	- Roll them around evenly and thinly coat all sides
	- Remove excess flour
5. Heat up oil in a pan over medium-high heat
	- Oil should cover the entire surface
	- First, check if the oil is ready
		- Place a test piece of tofu
		- Bubbles should appear *straight* away
	- Gently put in rest of tofu
	- The tofu pieces should not be touching! Leave space
6. Use chopsticks/tongs to flip tofu to ensure each side is evenly fried
7. Transfer to a plate lined with paper towels on both sides, to absorb excess oil
8. If frying vegetables (e.g. bell peppers), or spices (e.g. garlic), drain some of the oil and cook it in the residual
9. Enjoy!

Based on this [salt and pepper tofu recipe](https://redhousespice.com/salt-and-pepper-tofu/).
